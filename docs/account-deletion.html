Inspector's Path - Account Deletion Request

Developer: Inspector's Path Team
Last updated: May 24, 2026

This page explains how users of the Inspector's Path mobile app can request deletion of their app account and associated data.

How To Delete Your Account In The App

Open app -> Profile -> Delete Account -> Confirm Delete

After confirmation, your account deletion request is submitted and your session is logged out.

How To Request Deletion Outside The App

If you cannot access the app, email us from your registered email address at: contact@inspectorspath.com

Use subject line: Account Deletion Request - Inspector's Path

Data Deleted

  • Profile data (name, email, phone, avatar)
  • Account credentials and authentication records
  • Learning and exam activity linked to your account
  • Referral profile and related app account data

Data That May Be Retained

We may retain limited records when required for legal, tax, fraud prevention, security, dispute handling, or regulatory compliance. Retained records are minimized and protected.

Processing Timeline

We aim to process account deletion requests within 30 days of verification. If extra time is required due to legal/compliance review, we will notify you.

Contact

For questions about account or data deletion, contact contact@inspectorspath.com.
